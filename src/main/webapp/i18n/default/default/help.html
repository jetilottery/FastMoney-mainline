Fast Money

Experience the wealth of Fast Money raining down on you! Match numbers to win prizes. Sparkling Diamonds reward instant wins and maybe even double the win! Bursting with two lavish bonus rounds full of extravagant prizes: spin your luck in the Wheel Bonus, where your prize is multiplied up to 10x, or reveal and collect cash prizes within the Money Bags in the Prize Bonus.

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Match any Your Number to any Winning Number and win the corresponding prize.

Reveal a Diamond symbol to instantly win the corresponding prize.

Reveal a 2x Multiplier symbol to double the corresponding prize.

Find a Wheel Bonus symbol to trigger the Wheel Bonus.

Find a Prize Bonus symbol to trigger the Prize Bonus.

In the Wheel Bonus, press Spin to spin the wheel and reveal a prize value and a corresponding multiplier between 1x and 10x.

In the Prize Bonus, reveal the prizes hidden within the Diamonds. Revealing the gold star ends the game and collects the total of the revealed prize amounts.

In the Prize Bonus, select the Money Bags to reveal prizes. Reveal COLLECT to end the game and collect the total of the revealed prize amounts.

The game ends when all Winning Numbers, Your Numbers, and any triggered bonus items are revealed.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game's outcome.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

In this game, the wheel is a graphical animation for entertainment purposes only and does not represent an actual wheel. The chances of obtaining a specific segment are therefore not equiprobable.
